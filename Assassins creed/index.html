We work in the dark, to serve the light. We are Assassins.

The central premise of the story involves the rivalry between two ancient secret societies: the Assassins, who desire peace through free will, and the Templars, who seek to dominate the world and impose order on humanity.

Both had an indirect relationship with a species that lived before humans, whose society was destroyed by a gigantic solar storm. Mixing characters and historical fiction with real events and figures, the chronological order of the games begins in 2012, and tells of Desmond Miles, a young man who, with the help of the Animus (a machine that allows him to see his "ancestral memories"), explores the memoirs of some of the most prominent Assassins in history.

The animus serves as the conduit between the past and the present. Those who inhabit the device can connect to their ancestors through their genetic code. To this day, Assassin's Creed protagonists still use this device to experience the life of warriors from the past.
